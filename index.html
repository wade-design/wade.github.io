Selected Projects

Health Behavior Change

Handwashing with Ananse

UNICEF Ghana • Lead Game Designer

A culturally-grounded play-based curriculum that generated significant, measurable increases in handwashing with soap among Ghanaian primary-school children. Based on traditional Ananse folklore with songs, dance, and games. Now used nationwide in every Ghanaian elementary school.

Hygiene with Chhota Bheem

UBS Optimus Foundation • Design Team Lead

A multi-format curriculum driving behavior change around toilet use and handwashing in Tamil Nadu, India. Designed physical games, a mobile game, songs, and animated stories featuring beloved Indian cartoon character Chhota Bheem.
